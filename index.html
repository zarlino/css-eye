CSS Eye Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how CSS Eye ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection

CSS Eye does not collect any personal information. The extension works entirely locally within your browser.

Data Usage

CSS Eye only processes CSS and HTML data from the websites you visit for the purpose of analyzing and displaying CSS properties. This data is:

  • Processed entirely within your browser
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • Not stored permanently beyond the current browsing session

Permissions

CSS Eye requires the following permissions:

  • Access to website content (to analyze CSS)
  • Storage (to save your preferences)

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@css-eye.com
